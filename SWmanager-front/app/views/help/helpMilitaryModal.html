Help : Military

Search best Target:

You can search new Targets by selecting some fields. We will recommend the best for you.

You have to select :
- the planet where you wanna launch your fleet.
- the range of distance you want. Choose between 0 and 60 000.
You can choose :
- the status of player near you. #Inactif means that the players didn't connect since 15 days.
- the type of player near you.
- the score of player near you depending on your own score.

#TIPS# Distance:
- between 2 galaxy = 20.000 (no matters of system or planet position)
- between 2 system in same galaxy is > 2700 but < 48.000 (no matters of planet position)
- between 2 planet in same system is > 1000 but < 2000
#TIPS# Player type:
- Builder : player who has a lot of building. Good choice to steal resources easily.
- Bunker : player who has a large amount of defense. Good choice if you want make an hype action.
- Raider : player who has large amount of fleet. Good choice if you want make a HOF.
