Help : Colonial

Area Chart data:

You can see the repartition of the population across the galaxy. The range for data selection is every 100 systems.
- You can search by galaxy.

Polar data:

You can see the total percent of the population for each galaxy.
- You can click on elements to see the repartition in the Area Chart.

Search a new Colony:

You can search where to place a new colony by selecting some fields. We will recommend the best for you.

You have to select :
- the galaxy. If you want a colony in a specific galaxy, else choose 'All'.
- the amount of people. You can choose to have a lot or a few of player near you.
You can choose :
- the status of player near you. #Inactif means that the players didn't connect since 15 days.
- the type of player near you.
- the score of player near you.

#TIPS# Player type:
- Builder : player who has a lot of building. Good choice if you don't want enemies.
- Bunker : player who has a large amount of defense. Good choice if you don't want to be quiet..
- Raider : player who has large amount of fleet. Dangerous choice.
